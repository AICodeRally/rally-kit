# Brainstorm — Idea Generator

Help a stuck team come up with a business idea.

## Instructions

1. Read `.team-track` — tailor suggestions to their track

### Brainstorm Flow

Say: "Let's brainstorm! I'll ask a few quick questions to spark ideas."

**Ask these ONE AT A TIME (wait for each answer):**

1. "What's something that annoys you in daily life? A problem you wish someone would solve?"

2. "Think about your hobbies, your job, your classes — what tools do you WISH existed?"

3. "If you could build an app for someone you know (friend, family, coworker), what would it do?"

### After 3 answers, generate 3 options:

Based on their answers, propose exactly 3 concrete app ideas. Format:

> **Option 1: [App Name]**
> [One sentence — what it does and who it's for]
>
> **Option 2: [App Name]**
> [One sentence — what it does and who it's for]
>
> **Option 3: [App Name]**
> [One sentence — what it does and who it's for]
>
> **Which one sounds the most exciting? Or want me to come up with more?**

### Rules:
- Keep ideas BUILDABLE in 2 hours with mock data (no real APIs, no auth, no databases)
- Suggest ideas that work well with the component library (stat cards, tables, charts, forms)
- Make ideas specific to their track (Campus AI = student life, Startup = business tools, Future = career tools)
- If they can't pick, say: "Go with your gut — there's no wrong answer. We can always adjust as we build."
- Once they pick, transition naturally into Phase 1 design questions from CLAUDE.md
